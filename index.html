He was the first president of Ghana and a prominent advocate for African unity and independence from colonial rule. Nkrumah played a significant role in Ghana's independence from British colonial rule in 1957 and was instrumental in shaping the country's early years. He was a strong proponent of African socialism and sought to modernize Ghana's economy and infrastructure..
